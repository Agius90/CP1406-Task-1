Goals

The primary objective of this website is to increase sales in consumes, both new and secondhand, and to increase attendance to CossiePlay parties.

Success Evaluation

Costume sales and party attendee numbers will be audited on a monthly basis. If a steady increase in sales/attendees is observed, the website will be considered a success.

Target Audience

Males and females, aged 15-30 are the primary target audience. The website will be designed with this in mind, focusing on a clean look with easy and fun navigation.
